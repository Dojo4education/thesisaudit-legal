## How to delete your account in the app
Account deletion is available directly in the app:

1) Open the side menu  
2) Tap **Settings**  
3) On the settings screen, tap **Delete account**  
4) Confirm deletion of all data

After confirmation, the account is deleted and the userâ€™s data is removed from our systems.

## What data is deleted
When you delete your account, we delete all user-related data, including:
- account data (e.g., email address);
- user-uploaded materials/files and processing results;
- related records and technical identifiers required for the app to function.

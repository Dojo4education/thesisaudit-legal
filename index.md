## ℹ️ About ThesisAudit

**ThesisAudit** is an educational application designed to help students prepare for thesis defense by:
- generating AI-based questions,
- recording and analyzing answers,
- producing structured feedback reports.

Authentication is performed via:
- **Email**
- **Telegram account**

No public registration or anonymous access is provided.

## About ThesisAudit

**ThesisAudit** is an educational application for self-checking and learning support:
- generating questions based on user-provided study materials,
- recording and analyzing answers,
- producing structured feedback reports.

Authentication options:
- **Email + password**
- **Telegram (via bot start link)**
